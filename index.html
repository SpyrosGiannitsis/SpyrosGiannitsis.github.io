Help Page

This page allows you to view the weather forecast for different cities in Greece.

Instructions:

  • Enter the name of the city in Greece you want the weather for.
  • Select the forecast duration (24 hours, 3 days, or 7 days).
  • Click "Get Weather" to see the forecast, including temperature and wind speed.
  • Use the "Clear Page" button to reset the inputs and clear the map and charts.
